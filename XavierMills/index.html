PRODUCTS

Kasai
Pometia spp

Medium Hard Wood

Color/Appearance: Kasai heartwood, which is pink red or red-brown. Grain is straight or shallowly interlocked. Texture is moderately coarse and even

Common Uses: Suitable for beams, posts, joists, rafters, door and window frames, boat construction, tool handles, plywood, heavy-duty flooring, furniture and interior finishing.

Kempas
Koompassia malaccensis

Medium Hard Wood

Color/Appearance: Kempas tends to be an orangish-brown, with an overall mahogany-like appearance.

Common Uses: Flooring, heavy construction, railroad crossties, plywood, and pallets (throughout its natural range).

Tulang
Koompassia excelsa

Hard Wood

Color/Appearance: Tualang is dark red when fresh cut, changing to a deep chocolate-brown color with time. The species has a pronounced interlocked grain, with a coarse and generally even texture.

Common Uses: Flooring, heavy construction, railroad crossties, plywood, and pallets (throughout its natural range).
